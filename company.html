About TPCODL

TPCODL (TP Central Odisha Distribution Limited) is a joint venture between Tata Power and the Government of Odisha. It is responsible for electricity distribution in central Odisha.

The company is committed to delivering reliable and sustainable power supply and implements modern technologies for distribution management and customer services.
